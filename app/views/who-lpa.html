Who is the donor for this LPA?

The 'donor' is the person appointing other people to make decisions on their behalf.

The donor:

  • Must be 18 or over
  • must be able to make their own decisions when they create the LPA, known as having mental capacity
  • Only the donor can make decisions about their LPA and the people to involve
