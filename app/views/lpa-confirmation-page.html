What happens next

We have sent your LPA to the Office of the Public Guardian for registration.

Registration usually takes up to 20 weeks. During this time the Office of the Public Guardian will:

  • check your LPA is legally correct
  • notify the donor and any named people
  • register the LPA if there are no objections

Once registered, your LPA will be returned to you by post. You will need to print, sign and keep it in a safe place.

If you need help

Contact the Office of the Public Guardian if you have any questions about your application.
